    Event Details

    1. BGMI

    Gather your squad for the ultimate battle in BGMI (Battlegrounds Mobile India) where the stakes are high and victory is paramount.

    Competition Format
    • Event Time: 8:30 AM
    • Total Matches: 3 rounds
    • Maps: Erangel, Miramar, Livik or sanhok
    • Winner Determination: Total points accumulated over 3 matches

    Prizes:

    Winner Squads: Medals, Certificate of Achievement, and Surprise Prizes.


    2. Stumble Guys

    Join the chaos in Stumble Guys as you navigate through obstacles and race towards victory in this hilarious multiplayer mayhem.

    Competition Format
    • Event Time: 9:30 AM
    • Maps Selection: Driving or Race section.
    • Rounds: Based on participant count. Top 50% progress to the final round.
    Prizes:

    Top 3 Winners: Medals, Certificate of Achievement, and Surprise Prizes.


    3. Bug Buster - Python or C++ Error Fixing Challenge

    Prepare to put your debugging skills to the test in the Bug Buster - Python or C++ Error Fixing Challenge! This competition is perfect for Python and C++ enthusiasts who enjoy the thrill of troubleshooting and fixing errors.

    Competition Format
    • Event Time: 10:30 AM
    • Round 1 (30 minutes): Participants will be presented with a series of Python or C++ code snippets containing errors. Your task is to identify and fix these errors within the given time frame of 30 minutes.
    • Round 2 (20 minutes): Only the top 60% of participants from Round 1 will advance to Round 2.
    Prizes:

    Top 3 Winners: Medals, Certificate of Achievement, and Surprise Prizes.


    4. Code Canvas - Web Designing Competition

    Get ready to showcase your creativity and coding skills in the Code Canvas - Web Designing Competition! This competition is tailored for those passionate about web development and design.

    Competition Format:
    • Event Time: 11:30 AM
    • Round 1 (30 minutes): Participants will be tasked with completing a series of web design challenges within a time limit of 30 minutes.
    • Round 2 (20 minutes): Only the top 60% of participants from Round 1 will advance to Round 2.
    Prizes:

    Top 3 Winners: Certificate of Excellence, Medals, and Surprise Gifts.



    FAQ
    Q: Is there an entry fee for participating?

    A: Yes, the entry fee is ₹50, which allows participation in competitions.

    Q: How can I register for the event?

    A: Registration details have been provided on the register button!

    Q: What is the deadline for registration?

    A: The deadline for registration is one day before the event starts. Please register at least 4 hours before the event starts to secure your spot.

    Q: Where should we pay for registration?

    A: Payment will be accepted offline at the location of the event. Please ensure to make your payment at the registration desk upon arrival.

    Q: Can I be late by 5 minutes?

    A: We strongly advise all participants to arrive at least 30 minutes before the event start time. This will allow us to complete the registration process, allocate slots, and address any last-minute requirements efficiently.

    Q: Can I make payment online?

    A: For your convenience, cash payment will be preferred at the registration desk. Please bring the required amount in cash for registration.

    Q: What if I have further questions?

    A: If you have any additional questions or concerns, feel free to reach out to our event organizers at contact technotsav2k24@gmail.com / 9137899536. We're here to assist you!

    Q: How can I get event updates?

    A: If you haven't registered yet, please proceed to the registration button. Once registered, you'll receive details about joining the WhatsApp group for event updates.

    Q: How can I win in Bug Buster?

    A: If you're familiar with Python or C++, focus on understanding common errors and debugging techniques in these languages. Practice debugging code snippets to identify and fix errors efficiently. Stay calm and methodically analyze the provided code during the competition.

    Q: How can I win in Code Canvas?

    A: To excel in the Web Designing Competition, learn the fundamentals of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Practice creating various web components such as login/register forms, navigation bars, headers, footers, containers, and cards. Focus on creating visually appealing and functional web pages.

    Q: How can I win in Stumble Guys?

    A: Play strategically and safely in Stumble Guys. Be mindful of obstacles and avoid risky maneuvers that could lead to falling. Maintain your balance and utilize power-ups wisely. Remember, staying focused and playing smart can help you outlast the competition!

    Q: How can I win in BGMI?

    A: Prepare your squad by practicing together and developing effective communication strategies. Stay physically and mentally prepared by staying hydrated and energized. Focus on securing advantageous positions in the game and aim to come out on top by utilizing teamwork and strategic gameplay.
