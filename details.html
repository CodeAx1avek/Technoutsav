Code Canvas - Web Designing Competition

Get ready to showcase your creativity and coding skills in the Code Canvas - Web Designing Competition! This competition is tailored for those passionate about web development and design.

Competition Format:

  • Round 1 (30 minutes): Participants will be tasked with completing a series of web design challenges within a time limit of 30 minutes.
  • Round 2 (20 minutes): Only the top 60% of participants from Round 1 will advance to Round 2.

Prizes:

Top 3 Winners: Certificate of Excellence, Trophy, Medals, and Surprise Gifts.

Bug Buster - Python Error Fixing Challenge

Prepare to put your debugging skills to the test in the Bug Buster - Python Error Fixing Challenge! This competition is perfect for Python enthusiasts who enjoy the thrill of troubleshooting and fixing errors.

Competition Format:

  • Round 1 (30 minutes): Participants will be presented with a series of Python code snippets containing errors. Your task is to identify and fix these errors within the given time frame of 30 minutes.
  • Round 2 (20 minutes): Only the top 60% of participants from Round 1 will advance to Round 2.

Prizes:

Top 3 Winners: Trophy, Medals, Certificate of Achievement, and Surprise Prizes.
